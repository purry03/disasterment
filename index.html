Arena Layout

  • The arena consists of one main mobile depot, surrounded by four mobile depots around it at a radius of 300 kms.
  • The main depot (X) is the warp in point for the players
  • Players may freely warp to any depots after the match starts

Rules

All contestants are required to follow the following to rules to ensure fair play. Violation of rules will result in an instant defeat for that match without any warnings. Make sure you read and understand these rules.

Allowed Ships

  1. Only Tech 1 frigates are allowed from the following factions:
    • Caldari
    • Amarr
    • Minmatar
    • Gallente
  2. Navy/Faction or Tech 2 frigates are not allowed.
  3. All ships must only by fitted with Tech 1 modules/rigs/ammo/drones. Meta modules are not allowed.
  4. Any kind of implants or drugs are not allowed

Match Rules

  1. Initial warp in point is the center depot.
  2. Initial warp range can is 0 - 35 kms from the beacon. Failure to warp within range will cause your ship to be placed at 0 on the depot.
  3. After initial warp in, the match will start after a countdown of 5 seconds.
  4. Participants are not allowed to move their ship or lock each other before the end of the countdown.
  5. During the match, you are allowed to warp freely to any of the depots within the arena.
  6. If at any point a contestant warps outside of the area for any reason, it will result in an instant defeat.
  7. All kinds of fits and combat styles are allowed as long as they adhere to the rules above.
  8. During the match, the participants will be "Server Deafened" and "Server Muted" so that they cannot hear anyone else in the channel.
